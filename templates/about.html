About Foodoraa 🌿

Foodoraa is a smart food donation and delivery platform designed to reduce food waste and help people in need.

🍱 Hotels, restaurants, and event organizers can donate excess food easily.
🚴 Volunteers collect and deliver food to needy people and earn delivery income.
📊 Admin can track donations, deliveries, and system activity in real time.
🌍 The mission is simple: Reduce Food Waste + Help Society + Earn for Volunteers

Foodoraa acts like a digital bridge between excess food and hunger, ensuring no food is wasted.
