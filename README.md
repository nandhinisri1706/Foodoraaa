# Foodoraaa
Foodora is a smart food-sharing and community support app designed to reduce food waste and help people in need. The app connects restaurants, volunteers, NGOs, and individuals so that surplus food can be donated instead of wasted.

With an easy-to-use interface, users can donate extra food, request food support, track nearby donations, and coordinate pickups through volunteers. Foodora promotes sustainability, social responsibility, and community care by ensuring good food reaches the right people at the right time.

Key Features
🍱 Surplus food donation system
📍 Real-time food availability tracking
🙋 Volunteer pickup & delivery support
🏢 NGO and community organization collaboration
🔔 Instant notifications for nearby donations
🌱 Eco-friendly mission to reduce food waste
📊 Donation history and impact tracking

Foodora’s mission is simple:
“Save Food. Share Happiness.”
